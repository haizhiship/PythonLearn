A short history o the company

World Wide Spam was started in the summer of 2000.The business concept was to ride the dot-com wave and to make money both through bulk email and by selling canned meat online. After receiving several complaints from customers who weren't satisfied by their bulk meal,World Wide Spam altered their profile, and focused 100% on canned goods. Today, they rank as the world's 13,892nd online supplier of SPAM.
